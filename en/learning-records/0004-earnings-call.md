# Learning Record 0004 — Strategic Value of Earnings Calls

**Date**: 2026-06-16
**Lesson**: 0004-earnings-call.html
**Status**: Completed

## Key insights

1. **Prepared Remarks are a rehearsed performance; Q&A is the unscripted real conversation**
2. **Changes in guidance wording precede numerical changes** -- "confident" → "cautious" often predates actual numbers being cut by 1-2 quarters
3. **Guidance withdrawal is worse than bad numbers** -- it means management cannot see the future either (visibility = 0)
4. **What analysts ask reveals more about market concerns than what management answers**
5. **"Overall tone" is the most underrated judgment** -- numbers look fine but "something feels off" → trust your instincts

## Reading order for EC summary (5 min)

1. Financial highlights (30s) → 2. Guidance changes (1min) → 3. Key Q&A exchanges (2min) → 4. Risk disclosures (1min) → 5. Overall judgment (30s)
