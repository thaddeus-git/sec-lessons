## Key insights

1. **SEC filings are free, legally mandated competitive intelligence** -- more accurate than news, more objective than research reports, more reliable than private channels. This is the theoretical foundation of the entire competitor monitoring system.
2. **Three core forms**: 10-K (annual, most comprehensive), 10-Q (quarterly, trend tracking), 8-K (event-driven, fastest access to strategic moves)
3. **10-K reading order**: Item 1A (Risk Factors) → Item 7 (MD&A) → Item 8 (Financial Statements) → Item 1 (Business Description)
4. **8-K is the most underrated intelligence source** -- if you could only monitor one form, choose 8-K, because it covers "unexpected events" beyond 10-K/Q
5. **Chinese stocks (UXIN, ATHM) use different forms**: 20-F instead of 10-K, 6-K instead of 8-K
